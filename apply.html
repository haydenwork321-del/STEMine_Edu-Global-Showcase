The Process

Three steps to
global recognition

01

Submit Your Work

Fill out our Google Form. Be specific — this is a selective program. Submit a video, written piece, artwork, or animation.

02

We Review and Select

Our team carefully reviews every submission. Selected contributors are notified and their work is prepared for the global platform.

03

Go Live Globally

Your work is featured on STEMina's Global Showcase — seen by students, educators, and researchers worldwide. You receive your official Recognition Award.

Before You Apply

Submission criteria

Step 1

Submission

Accepted submission types

  • Research articles
  • STEM projects
  • Cultural STEM knowledge
  • Presentations (PDF format)
  • Educational videos
  • Infographics
  • Artwork (drawing, painting, poems, or any art form) / animations related to STEM

Required information

  • Country (optional)
  • Project title
  • Description (word count to be confirmed)
  • STEM connection
  • Cultural significance (optional)
  • Sources
Step 2

Reviewing process

Originality

  • Uses creative experimentation instead of a standard presentation
  • Avoids copying existing work
20%

STEM related

  • Relates to one or more of the following fields of study: science, technology, engineering, or math
25%

Educational value

  • Highlights a relevant topic
  • Has real-world applications
20%

Cultural respect / Authenticity

  • Accurately represents individuality and culture without stereotypes
  • Demonstrates appreciation for diverse perspectives and knowledge
15%

Organization

  • Good use of visuals and tools
  • Displays information in a clear format
  • Structured, logical flow of content (e.g. state a problem, then explain the "so what?")
10%

Sources

  • Uses reliable references
  • Credits any information, image, or data that is not their own

Note: if your entry violates the code of conduct, it will automatically be rejected.

Step 3

Code of conduct

Respectful

  • Respect different cultures, perspectives, and experiences
  • The inclusion of profane language is not permitted

Original work

  • Content must be original; plagiarism and AI-generated work will be flagged
  • Collaborators are permitted if they have been given credit in the entry

Safety / protection of privacy

  • Do not share personal or confidential information about yourself or others
  • Receive consent from other individuals if you decide to include an image of them
It is unacceptable to share hateful, copyrighted, or private content. Any behaviour that undermines the safety or integrity of this event will be immediately rejected.
