Made something for the board?

Drawings and STEM-inspired art from students around the world get pinned up here. Here's how to get yours added.

How It Works
  1. 01Submit a photo of your drawing or artwork via the Google Form below, along with your name and the STEM topic it's inspired by.
  2. 02Our team reviews it and prepares it for the board.
  3. 03It gets pinned up for everyone to see — with your name credited as the artist.
What We're Looking For
  • Original artwork — drawn, painted, or created by you
  • Clearly inspired by a STEM concept, cultural science fact, or traditional method
  • A clear photo or scan (good lighting, no blur)
  • Photo file under 3MB, with your drawing centered — the board displays it cropped to a 4:3 frame
Submit via Google Form

This is a selective program — be specific in your submission! No need for a high-resolution scan — the board displays drawings fairly small, so a regular phone photo works great.
