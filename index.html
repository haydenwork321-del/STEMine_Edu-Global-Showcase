Who We Are

Rewriting the story of accessible education

We're STEMina — a youth-led nonprofit that noticed a disparity in accessible education across the world. Many children don't receive the same level of education as others. We're here to change that by building a global community advocating for accessible STEM education.

Our Vision

Eliminate geographical barriers. Make STEM free and accessible to every child, everywhere.

Free Online Courses

Education tools available to any child pursuing STEM — no cost, no limits.
