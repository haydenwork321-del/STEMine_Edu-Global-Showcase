Got a STEM fact from your culture?

Science facts and traditional methods from around the world get pinned to this map. Here's how to get yours added.

How It Works
  1. 01Submit your fact via the Google Form below — include the country it's from and a short write-up.
  2. 02Our team reviews it for accuracy and fit, then approves it for the map.
  3. 03It goes live on the map for the world to explore — with your name credited as contributor.
What We're Looking For
  • A real science fact or traditional method tied to a specific country or culture
  • Accurate and, where possible, a source or explanation of how you know it
  • Written clearly enough to fit in a short map popup
